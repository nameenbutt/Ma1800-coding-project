Notebook Entry 02

She wrote dates only when she could bear to. Some were circled in red ink. Some were scratched out until the paper thinned.

"Not every year was survivable

  • 1947: She wrote: "A beginning. A wound A hope."
  • 1965: A note glued beside it read: "Home was quieter for a moment."
  • 1971: A torn photograph slipped between the pages.
  • 2001-present She wrote: "They waited for peace. It waited too."

One corner of the page had a hidden phrase. If tapped carefully, it opened her thoughts on faith
