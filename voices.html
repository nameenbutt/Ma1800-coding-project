Voices on the Margins

Notebook Entry 03

Between the lines of her diary, she taped small audio notes: a boy recalling the night the sky turned orange an old man speaking the name of every street he lost. a mother reciting dua in the hallway.

She wrote: "Their voices should not disappear. If i cannot keep them safe, i can keep them here."

In the fold between two pages, she left a scrap that simply said: "Do you hear them too?"
