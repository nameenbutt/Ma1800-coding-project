Faith & Fire

A Digital Journey Through War And Religion In Pakistan

A cinematic web experience designed to explore home how religion and war shaped life in Pakistan. Scroll through stories, listen to voices, and navigate a digital journey of survival, spirituality and history.

This project brings together photographs, spoken audio, archived news, and interactivw web-art to reveal personal and emotional perspectives beyond headlines, through immersive storytelling.
