Notebook Entry 01

She kept a small notebook during the years of unrest. In its pages, she wrote about the days that changed the country

Some entries were hopeful. Some were warnings. Some were prayers under her breath.

On the first page, she glued a picture of a mosque where the dust settled slowly after every explosion.

There were also fragments written sideways along the margins:

"They said the smoke rose for hours."

"The prayers still reached us."

"Nothing stayed quiet for long."

She didn't expect anyone to read these pages, but you found them here - in her voices
